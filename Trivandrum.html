TRIVANDRUM

Thiruvananthapuram or Trivandrum (English: Thiruvananthapuram) is the capital city of the state of Kerala and the headquarters of the Thiruvananthapuram district . It is also known as Ananthapuri . The city is located on the western coast of the Indian subcontinent at the southern tip of Kerala. Thiruvananthapuram is a city with a lot of geography, busy streets and commercial areas. As a result of India as the "green city" Mahatma Gandhi described here [2] . 2011 CensusAccording to the report, 957,730 people live within the municipal limits. According to this, Thiruvananthapuram is the most populous city in Kerala. Thiruvananthapuram is the largest city in Kerala. There are several offices of the State Government and the Central Government in the city of Thiruvananthapuram. It also houses the offices of some of the best private industrial chains in the world. Apart from being a political hub, Thiruvananthapuram is also home to many high quality educational institutions. The famous University , Rajiv Gandhi bio-technology research center , the Vikram Sarabhai Space Center , Thiruvananthapuram Engineering College , State College barttanhil enjaniyarinn, Indian Institute of Space Technology , Indian Institute of Science Education and Research , Thiruvananthapuram Medical College, RCC, Sree Chitra Thirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology are some of the notable ones. Techno Park, India's first IT complex , is located near Kazhakoottam in Thiruvananthapuram . It is also the headquarters of Keltron, one of the public sector undertakings in Kerala . Famous Padmanabhaswamy Temple , Attukal Bhagwati Temple , Palayam OTC Hanuman Temple , Pazhavangadi Ganapati Temple ,Srikanteshwaram Mahadev Temple , Palayam Juma Masjid and St. Joseph's Metropolitan Cathedral are located in the city.
